Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NullSix Studio collects, uses, stores, and protects information when people visit our public pages, contact the studio, or use software associated with the studio. The policy is written to support public trust, app review visibility, and a clearer understanding of how support and business communication are handled.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact details you voluntarily send to us, such as your email address, the message content, product identifiers, device context, and any supporting information you include in a support or business inquiry. We may also receive standard technical information such as browser type, approximate region, referral context, and page interaction data from hosting or analytics services.

How We Use Information

Information is used to answer support requests, review bug reports, manage commercial conversations, improve product and site quality, monitor the health of public pages, and maintain records related to moderation, abuse prevention, policy compliance, or publishing operations.

Device Permissions

If a product published by NullSix Studio requests device permissions, those permissions are expected to support features that are relevant to the software experience. The specific permission request and its use case should be presented inside the relevant app. We do not treat permission access as a blank check for unrelated data use.

Third-Party Services

Our sites or software may rely on third-party providers such as hosting, analytics, crash reporting, customer support tooling, advertising mediation, and ad network services. Those providers may process limited data under their own terms and privacy practices. We encourage users and partners to review the policies of any relevant third-party services connected to a specific product.

Data Retention

We retain contact and support records only for as long as reasonably necessary to answer requests, maintain issue history, support publishing obligations, resolve disputes, detect abuse, or satisfy legal and operational requirements. Retention periods vary according to the type of communication and the business need behind it.

International Transfers

Because online services, infrastructure providers, and product users may be located in different regions, the information associated with our public pages or products may be processed in countries other than your own. When that occurs, we aim to work with providers that offer reasonable safeguards for lawful data handling.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No internet-based transmission or storage environment can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but we still treat support and business communications as operationally important information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability for certain personal information. If you want to make a privacy-related request, contact us and include enough context for us to understand the relevant product or communication trail.

Children's Privacy

Our public site is not directed at children as a standalone service, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the site in a deliberate or unnecessary way. If you believe a child has provided information inappropriately, please contact us so we can review the situation.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through the main studio routes. For direct help, use support@nullsixstudio.com. For broader business-related coordination, use bussiness@nullsixstudio.com. General public routing details are also available on the contact page and the support page.
