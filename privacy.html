Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026. This policy explains how BrightSide Apps handles information across our website, support channels, and mobile products.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your email address when you contact support, as well as technical details like device type, app version, language, and usage diagnostics.

If one of our mobile products includes analytics, crash reporting, or account-light service features, those services may process limited operational information needed to keep the product working well.

How We Use Information

BrightSide Apps uses information to provide support, improve product reliability, respond to questions, maintain our website, and meet platform or legal obligations where required.

We aim to keep collection proportionate to the product need and avoid requesting more information than is necessary.

Ads and Analytics

Some products may use advertising or analytics tools to understand performance, measure product health, or support free access models. These services may use device-level or contextual signals permitted by the platform.

Where user consent or platform permissions are required, product behavior may adapt based on those settings.

Children and Age Guidance

We take age-sensitive use seriously. Where a product category or local requirement makes it necessary, we may apply stricter ad settings, reduced personalization, or additional safety handling for younger users.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or withdrawal of consent where applicable. We may ask for enough information to confirm and process a request responsibly.
