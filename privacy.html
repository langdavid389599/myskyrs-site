Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026. This policy explains how MySkyrs Studio collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information across our website, support channels, iOS apps, iOS games, and IAA monetization-related operations.

Scope and Controller

This Privacy Policy applies to MySkyrs Studio public pages at myskyrs.com, our support and contact channels, and any iOS apps or iOS games that link to this policy. MySkyrs Studio acts as the controller for information processed through these services unless a third-party platform or provider acts under its own separate privacy terms.

The purpose of this policy is to help users, reviewers, and business partners understand what information may be processed, why that processing happens, and how support, monetization, analytics, and platform distribution expectations are handled.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Information you provide directly. This may include your email address, the contents of support or business messages, screenshots, product names, purchase context, and any other details you voluntarily send to help us understand an issue or request.

Technical and usage information. Depending on the product, this may include device type, operating system version, app version, language, country or region signals, crash diagnostics, basic interaction signals, or feature usage information.

Partner and platform data. If a product integrates analytics, attribution, advertising, fraud-prevention, or App Store / Google Play related tooling, those services may provide limited operational signals needed to support delivery, entitlement checks, campaign performance, or anti-abuse controls.

How We Use Information

MySkyrs Studio uses information to provide support, maintain and improve products, analyze stability, investigate abuse, understand feature usage, and operate business communication responsibly.

We may also use information to measure product performance, respond to user rights requests, provide contact follow-up, support IAA monetization operations, and meet legal or platform obligations where required.

Ads and Analytics

Some products may include analytics or advertising frameworks to understand performance, support free access models, and operate IAA monetization responsibly. Depending on the product, such integrations may process device-level, contextual, or event-based information allowed by the platform.

Advertising-related signals may be used for impression measurement, anti-fraud control, reward completion, frequency management, or non-personalized and contextual delivery. Where platform permissions or user consent settings apply, product behavior may be adapted accordingly.

We aim to integrate monetization with care so that ads support product sustainability without becoming disconnected from the overall user experience.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense of unrestricted independent resale. We may share limited information with service providers and platforms that support hosting, diagnostics, support handling, analytics, advertising, attribution, fraud prevention, app distribution, or legal compliance.

We may also disclose information where required by law, valid legal process, or to protect service integrity, users, or business operations. If a business restructuring, transfer, or acquisition occurs, information may be transferred under appropriate confidentiality and continuity expectations.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for support, product operation, fraud prevention, legal compliance, entitlement handling, accounting, or legitimate record-keeping. Retention duration may vary depending on the data category and operational need.

When information is no longer reasonably needed, we aim to delete, anonymize, or otherwise de-identify it where feasible.

International Transfers

Because our website, iOS apps, iOS games, and service providers may operate across more than one country, information may be processed internationally. When cross-border handling is involved, we aim to use practical safeguards, provider controls, and access limitations appropriate to the service context.

Device Permissions

Our apps may request device permissions only when required by product features. Depending on the app, this may include storage access, notification access, network-related permissions, or other platform-managed permissions needed for specific functions.

Users can usually manage permissions through device settings, although turning off a permission may limit the feature that depends on it.

Third-Party Services

Our services may include links to app marketplaces, analytics providers, advertising networks, attribution tools, external support channels, or other third-party platforms. Those providers operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

We encourage users to review the policies of those third-party services before relying on them for payments, account activity, analytics, or advertising interactions.

Children and Age Guidance

We take age-sensitive use seriously. When a product category, local rule, or platform expectation requires it, MySkyrs Studio may apply stricter advertising behavior, reduced personalization, or additional safety handling. We do not intend to knowingly collect inappropriate personal information from children where parental consent or stricter safeguards are required.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent where applicable. We may request enough information to verify identity and scope before processing a request.
