Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including messages sent to support or business contact routes, and operational details such as device type, system version, app version, language, and crash or analytics signals.

If an iOS product or iOS game uses analytics, diagnostics, or monetization tooling, those systems may process the limited data needed to support product stability, usage analysis, and IAA monetization operation.

How We Use Information

MySkyrs Studio uses information to provide support, improve product performance, respond to business communication, maintain public pages, and operate commercial features responsibly across apps and games.

We aim to keep collection proportionate to the real product or service need and avoid unnecessary personal data use.

Ads and Analytics

Some products may use analytics or advertising frameworks to understand performance, measure retention, or support IAA monetization models. These integrations may process device-level or contextual signals allowed by the platform.

Where permissions, consent, or platform-specific privacy settings apply, product behavior may change based on those choices.

Children and Age Guidance

We take age-sensitive use seriously. When a product category, local rule, or platform expectation requires it, MySkyrs Studio may apply stricter advertising behavior, reduced personalization, or additional safety handling.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or withdrawal of consent where applicable. We may request enough information to verify and handle a request responsibly.
